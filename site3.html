Vizag Oil Drilling


Vizag Oil Drilling is an offshore oilfield 50 kilometres (31 mi) off the coast of Vizag, India, in about 75 m of water. The oil operations are run by Ace Oil and Gas Limited. Vizag Oil Drilling field was discovered by an Indian oil exploration team operating from the seismic exploration vessel Academic Arkhangelsky.
