Hyderabad Oil Refinery


The Hyderabad Refinery is a private sector crude oil refinery owned by Ace Oil and Gas Limited in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. The refinery was commissioned on 14 July 1999 with an installed capacity of 668,000 barrels per day (106,200 m3/d) later Increased to 1,240,000 barrels per day (197,000 m3/d). It is currently the largest refinery in the world.
